  • Where do we fly?

    Interjet connects more than 50 million passengers per year across 55 destinations within Mexico, United States, Canada, Latin America and the Caribbean with a modern fleet, operated by our committed and superb-trained staff. Check here our route map.

  • How much do I have to pay for baggage excess?

    Despite of the fare you choose, if you are carrying more than your checked baggage allowance, you’re entitled to take extra pieces of baggage upon payment of the applicable charge of $34 CAD per each bag. Please consider the maximum dimensions of special baggage and the weight restriction of 25 kg (55 lb) per piece. The total bag limit is 5 pieces (125 kg or 275 lb) per passenger.

    If any of your piece of baggage surpasses the maximum weight, you’ll be charged $7 CAD for each kilogram (2 lb) of extra weight, for a maximum of 5 kg (11 lb) per piece. That is, each bag can weigh up to 30 kg (66 lb).

    In case you exceed your checked baggage allowance, please arrive at least 3 hours before your flight at the airport check-in desk to verify availability within our cargo area.

    How many pieces and/or kg of baggage am I allowed to transport?

    In carry-on baggage, you can take 1 or 2 pieces of up to 10 kg.

    In checked baggage, you can transport up to 5 pieces of 25 kg (55 lb.) each.

    What are the restricted items in my baggage?

    You can check the list in the Restricted Items section.

    Are mobility aids subject to your baggage policies?

    Nor wheelchairs or mobility aids are subject to our baggage policy, which means that can be transported at no cost. However, due to space limitations, these items can only be transported as checked baggage.

    Battery-powered wheelchairs are allowed, if the battery is sealed to avoid leakage of its content (it is not necessary to insulate electrolyte-type batteries). Batteries must be disconnected and the terminals insulated to prevent accidental short-circuits.

    Do I need to inform that I require a wheelchair?

    Although you can take your own wheelchair on your trip, since mobility aids are required to travel in the baggage compartment, Interjet, upon request, can provide proper mobility assistance within the airport.

    Please contact our Customer Care Center at least 72 hours prior to your flight, to ask for this service. Please note that the service does not include help within washroom areas.

    The complete information about usability of wheelchairs and mobility aids can be provided at our Customer Care Center, sales offices or directly at airports. Please arrive at least an hour earlier of the suggested check-in time to ensure the proper handling and packaging of this type items.

    Can I take a car seat or stroller on-board the plane?

    No. These items can only travel as checked baggage (the service has no cost).

    For your comfort, strollers and car seats can be transported to the door of the plane, where you have to deliver them to our personnel, in order that they can be taken to the baggage compartment.

    Can I take musical instruments on-board the plane?

    If you’re taking musical instruments with you, they can fly as carry-on baggage if they are packed in a hard case that doesn’t exceed the following dimensions: 40 x 30 x 22 cm (15 x 12 x 9 in). The maximum allowed weight is 10 kg (22 lbs).

    If your musical instruments exceed the limits of the carry-on baggage, they will have to fly as checked baggage. The suitcase should not exceed 2 m (78 in) in length and weigh more than 30 kg (66 lbs).

    In case of delicate or voluminous musical instruments, they may occupy an additional seat, involving a full fare seat payment. If musical instruments are transported this way, they should not exceed 2 m (78 in) in length or weigh more than 46 kg (101 lbs).

    ¿Can I transport sports equipment?

    Due to space limitations, sports equipment can only be transported as checked baggage. Depending on the type of items you carry, it is important to consider the restrictions of weight and length, 30 kg (66 lb) and 138 in (3.50 m), in order to be transported by Interjet. Also, please take into account the specifications that you can find in the Special baggage section.

    You can find this information at the landing page of Problems with your baggage.

    What is the departure tax?

    It is a fee that passengers (including children under 2 years of age) who take a flight from some countries have to pay. The cost of this tax is included in the flight ticket.*

    *If you purchased a flight ticket from Costa Rica before November 2016, you will need to pay the departure tax directly at the airport. The cost of this fee is $29 USD.

    What is the TUA?

    It is the airport tax on flight tickets for the use of air terminals.

  • What are the differences between Light, Optima and Priority fares?

    Light Fare is perfect if you want to fly with little baggage. With this fare, as with the others, you will enjoy benefits like courtesy snacks and drinks, or a wide space between seats. Optima Fare allows you to make changes to your reservation at a lower price than Light Fare, besides, it gives you the chance to select your seat. Priority Fare lets you choose your seat between the 2 first rows of the airship; in addition, it offers preferential check-in and boarding, changes at no cost and reimbursements up to 24 hours before the flight. Check the benefits of each fare in detail here.

    What is the baggage limit in each fare?

    Every fare allows you to take 10 kg as carry-on baggage, divided in up to 2 pieces. On flights from Mexico to Canada or the United States and vice versa, Optima Fare lets you take one piece of checked baggage, while Priority Fare enables you to fly with 2.

    In domestic flights in Mexico or on flights from Mexico to Latin America and vice versa, Light Fare allows you to travel with one bag as checked baggage. Likewise, you can fly with 2 pieces in Optima Fare and with 3 in Priority Fare.

  • Can you request me to move to a different seat that the one that I bought?

    In Interjet, we respect the seat selection of our passengers, however, eventually, our personnel could request you to change your seat when boarding the plane for operational or security reasons. Here we mention some cases:

    Emergency exits occupied by persons with disabilities (temporal or permanent) or people who fly with an infant or a minor.

    Preferential seats for people with disabilities occupied by passengers without disabilities.

    Passengers who travel with a guide dog and require seats with enough space to place it.

    When our personnel consider that it is necessary to guarantee the security of passengers, cabin crew and the airship.

    Do you have preferential seats for people with disabilities?

    Yes. In Interjet, every airship has preferential places for people with disabilities. However, at the moment of the purchase, a person with a disability can choose to travel in any seat, except in those located near emergency exits.

    How can I get preferential seats for people with disabilities?

    In order to offer the best service, please inform us at the time of purchasing your ticket or with 24 hours of anticipation to the flight departure.

    In case that, at the moment of the request, there are no preferential seats available, you will have to go to the counter area at least 3 hours before the flight departure in national flights and 4 hours before in international flights.

    What happens with my seat reservations if I change the date and/or schedule of the flight?

    You will have to select your seat again.

  • How can I know if my purchase is confirmed?

    A ticket purchase is confirmed when Interjet delivers the confirmation code. Interjet issues the confirmation code when getting the total payment of the ticket. The code is sent to the email address provided in the contact data.

    Where can I find my reservation code?

    The reservation code appears on the screen when you complete the purchase through our web page and also appears in the confirmation email. This code can be checked on the website, by entering the passenger data, the point of departure and destination, and the email provided at the time of the purchase.

    Why I cannot do the web check-in?

    Online check-in is only available in the 48 previous hours of domestic flights. In international flights, it needs to be done at the airport counters.

  • What do I need to get my boarding pass?

    Passengers will have to go to the check-in desks with 2 hours of anticipation to the flight departure in national flights and 3 hours before in international flights. Likewise, they will have to show the following documents:

    In national flights, an official identification with photo.

    In international flights, a valid passport, in addition to the visa or the requested permits by the migration authorities of the destination country, in case they are needed.

    Other data that may be requested are: the reservation code, name and telephone number of a contact person and any other information needed to comply with the security provisions.

    What do I need to board the plane?

    You will have to go to the assigned boarding area at least:

    30 minutes before the departure time established in you boarding pass, in national flights.

    50 minutes before, in case of international flights.

    Likewise, you will have to show your boarding pass and a valid official identification to the airline personnel.

  • Once I made the purchase, can I change the name of the passenger?

    Yes. You can change the name on the webpage, or through the Call Center or at the sales offices, covering the corresponding amount plus taxes (it varies depending on the chosen fare).

    It is important to consider that if your flight is a round trip, you won’t be able to make changes in your reservation once you take the outbound flight.

    Can I make date and/or schedule changes to my flight?

    Yes. It is possible to modify the date and/or schedule of the flight, as long as:

    There is availability of space on the new flight.

    The corresponding payment is made.

    The cost between the paid fare and the fare in force at the moment of making the change is covered (if the current fare is lower than the one you paid, there won’t be any refund).

    The desired change is requested at least 4 hours before the departure of the flight originally booked, directly at the origin airport, at the sales offices, through the Call Center or in our website.

    Is it possible to change the destination of my flight?

    Yes. It is possible to modify the route, as long as:

    There is availability of space on the new flight.

    The corresponding payment is made.

    The cost between the paid fare and the fare in force at the moment of making the change is covered (if the current fare is lower than the one you paid, there won’t be any refund).

    The desired change is requested at least 4 hours before the departure of the flight originally booked, directly at the origin airport, at the sales offices, through the Call Center or in our website.

    What fare allows making changes to my reservation with no additional charge?

    Priority Fare gives the passenger right to make changes in departure times, flight date, name or route, with no additional charges, provided that such modifications are requested during the validity of the ticket and there is availability within the corresponding fare group.

    For purposes of the above, the passenger must request the desired change at least 4 hours prior to the departure of the originally contracted flight, directly at the airport of origin, or Call Center or sales office.

    If the requested change involves the application of a fare higher than the one originally paid, the passenger must pay the corresponding difference, both per segment and per passenger. Otherwise, that is, if the change involves the application of a fare lower than the one originally paid, the difference will be credited to the passenger in his/her electronic wallet, for which the passenger must have a Club Interjet and/or Club Interjet Premium account.

    On the other hand, in our 2 other fares -Light and Optima-, changes in reservations do have a cost. However, it is important to note that these modifications have a lower cost in Optima Fare.

  • Do I have to pay a fare to transport an infant (children under two years of age)?

    Infants don’t pay a fare and can travel in the arms of an adult; they neither have an assigned seat nor baggage allowance.

    Some countries charge a departure tax. In those cases, infants don’t pay a fare but have to cover that fee at the origin country.

    When an adult travels with infants, he/she has to inform Interjet at the time of the purchase. It is only permitted that an adult travels with 2 children under 2 years of age: while the first one could travel in the arms of his/her father, mother or guardian, the second infant has to pay a fare, which gives him/her the right to an assigned seat and baggage allowance.

    Do you have discounts on fares for children between 2 and 12 years old?

    Children who are 2 years of age or older pay a full fare.

    Can you transport an unaccompanied minor? What do I need?

    Yes. Interjet can transport unaccompanied minors who are between 5 and 18 years old minus one day, at the moment of the flight. This service has a cost of $68 CAD.

    The following considerations need to be taken into account:

    Unaccompanied minors cannot travel with Priority Fare due to the airline policies in relation to passengers with special needs.

    If a minor travels alone, Interjet needs to be informed at the moment of the purchase.

    At the time of checking-in, the father, mother or guardian will have to specifically request Interjet the transportation of the unaccompanied minor.

    The father, mother or guardian will have to provide the necessary information to fill the form “Request of Unaccompanied Minors Service” (data of the minor, data of the person who delivers the minor and data of the person who receives the minor at the destination airport). Besides, the person exercising parental authority or guardianship over the minor will have to sign this document.

    The father, mother or guardian will have to stay at the airport until the plane takes off.

    The person designated by the father, mother or guardian of the minor, will have to show an official identification with photo to receive the child at the destination airport.

    In addition to the passport of the minor, official ID of the person who gives authorization (the one who has parental authority or guardianship) and, if this happens, official ID of the adult who will accompany the minor, one of the following documents is requested when traveling abroad:

    • A document through which the father, mother or guardian, authorize the departure of the minor from the country, executed before a notary public.
    • A document issued by the competent authority. Minors with Mexican nationality or residency, who travel from Mexico to other countries, require the Departure Form for Minors (SAM). You can learn more about the process to get this document on the National Institute of Immigration (INM) website. If the document that contains the authorization is granted abroad, it must be legalized or apostilled, as appropriate, and be accompanied by the respective translation if written in any language other than Spanish.
