TERMS AND CONDITIONS

  1. Interjet staff verifies the weight and measurements of each piece. The cost for each excess kg is $5 USD.
  2. The choice of seat must be made within the same fare category and is subject to availability.
  3. The $8 USD charge is in addition to the cost of the flights and must be paid at the time of booking.
  4. The weight of each suitcase must not exceed 30 kgs (66 lbs). A maximum of 5 suitcases is allowed per passenger; these additional suitcases can be pre-purchased here.
  5. Sporting equipment, musical instruments and pets are exempt from payment as long as they are substituted for one of the allowed suitcases according to the fare chosen. When the suitcases exceed the number of permitted bags, a $25 USD charge will be assessed for piece, per segment. Pre-purchase special baggage here.
  6. lf you require any of these services, check the requirements for transportation here and contact our Customer Service Center for assistance.
  7. Changes will be granted as per last minute waiting list at the airport boarding gate and will be subject to availability in each corresponding cabin. This benefit will not apply for Optima fare tickets that were purchased with some type of promotion.
  8. The prices shown apply per section and per change. They must be made at least 4 hours before the departure of the flight and can be requested at our sales or airport offices or via our call center. Changes that involve adjustments in routes, dates and or schedules, will be subject to availability within the same fare category and may generate an additional cost in case of a fare difference. In addition, they will be subject to the baggage allowance policy in effect at the time of change. No changes are permitted if the passenger has already flown the first segment of the reservation.
  9. The refund will be made only via e-wallet and is applicable only if requested with at least 24 hours prior to the flight’s departure.
  10. The bonus between 2% and 15% will depend directly on the type of Club Interjet membership with which the booking was made. The bonus will be received via an e-wallet, and will be reflected one business day after completing the corresponding segment

* All services are subject to availability and are non-refundable. Total prices, including taxes are subject to change without notice. The final price will be shown at the time of booking or changes.
