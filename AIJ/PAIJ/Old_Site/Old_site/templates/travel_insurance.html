Travel insurance

Thinking about your peace, Interjet offers you an insurance supported by Seguros Atlas, which you can acquire when you buy your flight ticket. You will only spend time enjoying your vacation or closing an important deal.

This protection includes:

  • Up to MXN $6,500 on trip cancelations.
  • Up to MXN $7,000 on baggage loss.
  • Concierge service.
  • Roadside assistance.
  • Transmission of urgent messages.
  • Medical orientation by telephone.
  • Medical consultation to your home or place of accommodation (with cost for the customer).
  • Up to MXN $25,000 on economic aid for emergency expenses in case of accident of disease.
  • Up to MXN $100,000 in case of accidental death.
