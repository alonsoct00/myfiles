Living culture, pleasure and nature

Lima is Peru's largest city and its capital. Founded in 1535 by Francisco Pizarro and is actually a mosaic of many smaller cities. Comprising 43 districts with nearly 9 million inhabitants.

Museums with great works of art, beaches, natural reserves, archaeological sites, valleys, the nightlife, and the exquisite cuisine make tourism a unique experience in the country.

Larcomar has incredible ocean views, which you can enjoy from numerous restaurants offering several American franchises serving everything from hotdogs to ribs and ceviche. Fly to lima and discover all to make your stay a memorable experience...
