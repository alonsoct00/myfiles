On board services

Drinks and snacks

All our flights include courtesy alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages, among which are soda, juice, water, tea and coffee. In addition, with no extra cost, a wide selection of snacks is offered
(sweet, salty or sandwiches, depending on the flight duration).

Take-off/Landing camera

Experience a unique view of the most exciting moments of your flight. Thanks to the special cameras, strategically mounted under the fuselage, you can watch through our screens the take-off and landing of your flight in real-time.
Be there, first row!

Women designated restroom

The comfort of our passengers is essential for Interjet. For this reason, we established a women designated restroom in all their aircrafts. These restrooms, signaled with the pink symbol that illustrates this section, are shown during the flight by the cabin crew
