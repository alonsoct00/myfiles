Charters

Make the trip of your dreams with Interjet!

It doesn’t matter if you travel for leisure or business reasons, Interjet Charter Service allows you to book an airship and fly to any of our destinations in Mexico.

Interjet private flights give you more flexibility when you travel, without leaving aside the benefits that characterize us: flexible policies of baggage, courtesy snacks and drinks, and 20% more space between seats than the rest of Mexican high efficiency or low cost airlines.

If you need a quote, you can request it through our call center or writing an email to charters@interjet.com.mx, indicating schedule, destination and number of passengers. Besides, to guarantee the flight reservation, we recommend you to ask the quote 45 days before on flights within Mexico and 60 days before on international flights.
