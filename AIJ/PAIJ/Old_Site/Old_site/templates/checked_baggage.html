Are you carrying more bags or items than usual?

Interjet allows you to bring up to 5 pieces with the same size and weight limitations of 25 kg (55 lb) per piece. It its important to consider that
an additional $34 CAD fee will be apply for the extra piece you add to your checked baggage allowance.

In case you need to travel with a musical instrument, sport equipment or your pet, it can be transported at no cost only if you substitute one of your permited checked bags. The number of pieces of checked baggage vary depending on the fare you choose. Please refer to Our fares and Special baggage pages for more information.
